Exit Points is a free-improvisation concert held the last Wednesday of every month.
Two small ensembles each play a set, and then rearrange themselves, with guests, for very short pieces. Click on any of the show posters below to watch specific shows.
We also run Discord server to help the improvisation community with all things related to performing together online.
